Welcome to Online Examination System

In online examination system the exams are conducted using objective questions. The questions can have single answer or multiple answers. The online examination system tries it's best on providing all the features that an examination system must have.

Taxonomy

The online examination system is controlled by administrators, who take care of managing the users of the system, schedule, exams, evaluation, results, etc. Education are responsible for preparing the courses, preparing exam question and answers. Participant are responsible for attempting the exam.
